What We Do

We take care of the whole process, transforming your vision into beautiful growing trees. Sit back, dream big, and let us worry about the logistics.

Prepping ▼

  • We visit the site in order to plan out the project, gathering data on soil type and prevailing wind conditions to identify the best trees for your project.
  • Using AI imaging, we can show you how the project will look fully planted out, allowing you to amend it according to your vision.
  • We then pre-spray the site to optimise tree survival.

Planting ▼

  • We take care of organising the trees from local nurseries or trees we've grown. If you would prefer to supply the trees, however, we would be happy to plant them at a reduced cost.
  • We then schedule a time that works for you and get stuck into it.

Preserving ▼

  • We follow up after planting to ensure your satisfaction.
  • After six months, we come back again to make sure they’re all growing great.
  • Additionally, you can subscribe to a long-term follow-up plan at a fixed rate of $4.00 per tree plus travel costs.

Types of Plantings We Cover

Riparian Strips ▼

Riparian Strips are a great way to take care of your land. By planting trees and wetland shrubs, you can help nature do its job of filtering waterways and provide safe habitat for native flora and fauna. There is a lot of department help in this area, and we would be happy to put you in contact, as well as work with our plant experts to source the right trees for your unique wetland area.

Shelter Belts ▼

With developing weather conditions, establishing a strong shelter belt can be a great idea. This requires strong upright plants that can take the force of a powerful cold wind. Ensuring this is planted right for maximum protection is crucial for a functional shelter belt. This can be complex with power lines or even if you want to keep an eye on stock from the kitchen window, but with our AI imaging tools, we can ensure that the perfect trees are planted for that project.

Landscapes ▼

Trees can last for a very long time, and planting something garish that the wife’s going to give you grief about for the next 50 years might not be the best of ideas. With our AI imaging, however, we can provide you with a window to the future so that you can see how it will look, and more importantly, how it will look compared to the surroundings. We can also work with you to make sure that we get plants to the right height if you still want to see some stunning mountains in the back or keep an eye out for the postie up the road.

Native Reforistation ▼

Native tree reforestation is a movement that is gaining more support from government departments, but it is also a job that sorely needs to be done. If you have some low-yield land, converting it back to native bush can be a great way to beautify the land and help New Zealand’s unique native flora and fauna. We can work with nurseries to bring a diverse range of native trees and plants to bring back a little slice of original paradise. Depending on the size of the project, we can also potentially get some supplementary funding from the government and set you up for carbon crediting, transforming that land into not only a beautiful piece of native forest but also making it financially rewarding for you in the long run. Yes, money can grow off trees.

About Our Ai

About our ai simulation technology ▼

With the help of AI tools, we are able to take photos of the project site and then develop a realistic view of how the project will look, with the trees you want to see. This is a powerful tool, as it means you get an idea of what it will look like and whether you want to proceed or not. We can also make adjustments to the types of plants if they do not match your vision.

  • Before

    When we come out to visit you, we can take an image of the project, which is useful for estimating the number of trees required for the project and identifying size requirements. For landscape features, we can also ensure to capture the surroundings of the project if you wish the final product to be contrasting/complimentary in colour/texture to the surrounding environment.

  • After

    Using AI simulation technology, we can show you a realistic depiction of how the project will look. This allows you to visualise what it will be like 5-10 years down the road. Additionally, if you are not satisfied with the look of the project, we can play around with the spacing and types of trees to ensure that we create the project you want to see. We can also factor in the full height of the plants fully grown, as well as spread, in order to avoid shading, obscuring views, or other size-related issues.

  • Forever

    Partnering with Resolution Farming, we are able to keep a record of the trees that were planted on your site, as well as long-term survival rates. We can also, with your approval, keep track of soil type, moisture, and species type. This information can be used to help you with your farm plan, which we would be happy to assist you with. It also allows us to gather a more in-depth understanding of the intricacies of the process, refining and ever improving our service. None of the personal information we collect is distributed or shared, and if you prefer, we can refrain from collecting personal identifiers.
